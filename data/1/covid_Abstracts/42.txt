Since the first case of coronavirus disease19 (COVID-19) was reported in Wuhan, 
China, as of March 2, 2020, the total number of confirmed cases of COVID-19 was 
89,069 cases in 67 countries and regions. As of 0 am, March 2, 2020, the 
Republic of Korea had the second-largest number of confirmed cases (n = 4,212) 
after China (n = 80,026). This report summarizes the epidemiologic features and 
the snapshots of the outbreak in the Republic of Korea from January 19 and March 
2, 2020.
